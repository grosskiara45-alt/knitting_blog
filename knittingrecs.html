5 Free Spring/Summer Knitting Patterns

Date: April 7th 2026

With the weather finally warming up and the sun shining again, I know we are all excited to get our spring wardrobe out! For this blog, I wanted to share 5 free patterns that I think would be great for your spring/summer knits. Some of these patterns I am currently working on and some are ones that I would love to knit in the future! Hope you enjoy it!


1. Picnic Bandana

This simple yet fun bandana is great for the spring/summer! I recently decided to knit this and it quickly came together. If you have never done colorwork, this is a great pattern for getting your feet wet and diving into those techniques. Here is a photo of mine (I used navy blue and grey as the contrast colors)!


2. Summer Vines Vest

Of course, I had to recommend my current WIP! If you are like me and love to wear a light cardigan during the spring/summer, this pattern has been awesome to knit so far. DROPS Design makes the process of reading the charts and patterns super easy and also includes video tutorials for any techniques you are not familiar with. Check out my blog here


3. April Scrunchie

Even though I rarely wear my hair in ponytails, I think this cute scrunchie is perfect for spring/summer! The ruffles on the sample look incredible and seem like a fun technique to learn. I hope to knit this after my current WIP is at least halfway done!


4. Micro Shorts

Loupy’s Studios has some of the most creative knit designs I have seen so far and their micro shorts pattern has not only some fun techniques that I would love to learn but is also versatile! I’ve seen her use this pattern to also make capris and I would love to knit these in the summer! Check out her instagram here: https://www.instagram.com/loupystudio/


5. Tuttie Frutti Tee

This newly released pattern has been on my radar for a while as my goal is to knit a design like the small fruit on a piece before the end of the year. This tee is very adorable and Bea Creative Knits also has cool knitwear pieces that stand out!
